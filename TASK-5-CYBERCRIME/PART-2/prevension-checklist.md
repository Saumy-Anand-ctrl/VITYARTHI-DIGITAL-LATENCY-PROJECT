Stay Safe Online: A Checklist 

1.Use strong, unique passwords: Use a password manager to generate and store complex passwords.

2.Enable two-factor authentication (2FA): Add an extra layer of security to your accounts, especially email and banking.

3.Be cautious with links and attachments: Avoid clicking suspicious links or downloading unknown attachments.

4.Verify sender authenticity: Check the sender's email or phone number before responding or clicking links.

5.Use secure networks: Avoid public Wi-Fi for sensitive activities; use a VPN if necessary.

6.Protect financial info:Use UPI apps with extra security features like PINs and biometric locks.Never share UPI PINs or OTPs with anyone.

7.Monitor account activity: Regularly check bank and UPI transactions for suspicious activity.

8.Report cybercrimes: If you encounter a cybercrime, report it to National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in) and Helpline 1930.
